Grifff's Audiodrama Catalog

What is an audiodrama?

An audiodrama is a scripted, voice-acted, fiction podcast, similar to a TV show without the visual aspect. Audiodramas are a relatively niche medium dominated by small creators.

What is this website?

This website is a record of audiodramas I've listened to and my short review of them, designed to be easily searched. Ratings of audiodramas on this website are subjective to how much I enjoyed them, not how objectively good they are. I personally don't like horror audiodramas, but just about any other genre can be found here.
